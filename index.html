About me

Since September 2021, I am a ATER based at Sorbonne University. From January 2019 to August 2021, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Beijing International Center for Mathematical Research, working with Pingwen Zhang. From Janqu I did also a postdoc my Ph.D thesis in the University of Grenoble-Alpes under the supervision of Didier Bresch et Raafat Talhouk, which I defended the 19th December 2016.

Research interests

I am interested on the mathematical analysis of mathematical models arising from fluid dynamics equations. Here is a few keywords about my research: Incompressible and compressible fluids, Degenerate elliptic equations, Newtonian and non-Newtonian fluids, Functional inequalities.
