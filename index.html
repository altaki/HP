About me

Since September 2021, I am a ATER at Sorbonne University. Previously, I was employed by Beijing International Center for Mathematical Research at Peking University as a postdoc position under the supervision of Pingwen Zhang (Jan 2019 to Aug 2021, interrupted due to Covid-19), and by Sorbonne University as a ATER position (from Jan 2019 to Aug 2019), and by INRIA (from Sep 2017 to Dec 2018). I did my Ph.D thesis at the University of Grenoble-Alpes under the supervision of Didier Bresch and Raafat Talhouk, which I defended the 19th December 2016.

Research interests

I am interested in the mathematical analysis of mathematical models arising from fluid dynamics equations. Here is a few keywords about my research: Incompressible and compressible fluids, Degenerate elliptic equations, Newtonian and non-Newtonian fluids, Functional inequalities.
